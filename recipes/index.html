Jumbo Chocolate Chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 4 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups butter (3 full sticks)
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 12-ounces/2 cups of semi sweet chocolate chips or chunks

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375Â°

  2. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a medium sized bowl and set aside.

  3. In a large bowl mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until it has a creamy consistancy
  4. While still mixing add eggs and vanilla
  5. while mixing on a slow speed slowy incorperate the flour mixture. Make sure to scrape the sides of the bowl frequently
  6. finally mix in the chocolate chips/chunks use a wooden/plastic spoon make sure to mix evenly
